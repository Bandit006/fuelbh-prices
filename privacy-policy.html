Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Banzeen ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. Banzeen is owned and operated by Banzeen.

The short version

Banzeen does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your own device. We have no servers that hold your information, no accounts, and no tracking.

Information we collect

None. Banzeen does not ask for your name, email, location, or any other personal information. The cars you add and any calculations you make are stored only on your device using Apple's on-device storage. We cannot see this data.

Fuel prices

To show current fuel prices, the app downloads a small public file of official Bahrain fuel prices from the internet. This request does not include any personal information about you. It simply fetches the latest prices, the same way opening a public web page does.

Notifications

If you choose to turn on price change alerts, notifications are created on your own device. We do not run a notification server and we do not know whether you have alerts on or off.

Third parties

Banzeen does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or any third-party tracking tools. We do not sell or share data, because we do not collect any.

Children

Banzeen does not collect data from anyone, including children, and is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date at the top.

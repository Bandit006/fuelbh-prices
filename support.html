Frequently asked questions

Are the fuel prices live?

Prices in Bahrain are set monthly by the Fuel Price Committee. Banzeen shows the current official prices and updates automatically when they change. Always confirm the exact price at the pump.

How do I add my car?

Go to the My Cars tab, tap the plus button, then choose your brand, model, and engine. The tank size and fuel type fill in automatically. If your car is not listed, you can add it manually.

My car's fuel use looks different from real life.

The values in the app are typical figures. Real fuel use changes with driving style, traffic, and air conditioning. You can edit the numbers for your car under the Details section when adding or editing it.

Does Banzeen collect my data?

No. Everything stays on your device. Banzeen does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

How do I add the widgets?

On your iPhone home screen, press and hold an empty area, tap the plus button in the corner, search for Banzeen, and choose a widget.

How do I change the language or appearance?

Open the Settings tab in the app. You can switch between English and Arabic, and between light and dark mode.

About

Banzeen is an unofficial tool to help drivers in Bahrain check fuel prices and estimate trip costs. It is owned and operated by Banzeen.
